# LUMIÈRE CURVES - Business Plan
## Premium Plus-Size Lingerie E-Commerce Venture
### Founded by Ashjad & Ashnab | Legacy of Loviena Ties, Mumbai

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Brand Name:** Lumière Curves
**Tagline:** "Confidence in Every Curve"
**Industry:** Plus-Size Intimate Apparel
**Business Model:** D2C E-commerce + Amazon FBA
**Target Launch:** Q2 2025
**Initial Investment Required:** $45,000 - $75,000 USD

Lumière Curves is a premium plus-size lingerie brand that honors the family legacy of Loviena Ties while entering a high-growth market segment. The global plus-size clothing market is valued at **$315 billion in 2025** with intimate apparel being one of the fastest-growing segments at **5.3-8.67% CAGR**.

### 1.2 Target Market Demographics by Region

#### **PRIMARY MARKET: United States** (Recommended Launch Market)
- **Market Size:** $37.3 billion (plus-size clothing)
- **Women's Plus-Size Intimate:** ~$12.7 billion
- **Obesity Rate:** 40%+ of adults
- **Online Shopping Penetration:** 85%+
- **Average Order Value:** $65-95
- **Why US First:**
  - Largest addressable market
  - High e-commerce adoption
  - Strong body positivity movement
  - Premium pricing acceptance
  - Amazon FBA infrastructure

#### **SECONDARY MARKET: United Kingdom & Europe**
- **UK Market:** ~$8 billion plus-size segment
- **Key Countries:** UK, Germany, France
- **Consumer Behavior:** 40%+ prefer wireless/seamless
- **Premium Positioning:** Strong demand for luxury lingerie
- **EU Advantages:** Duty-free Bangladesh imports

#### **TERTIARY MARKET: Australia & Canada**
- Similar demographics to US
- Lower competition
- High purchasing power
- English-speaking (easier marketing)

### 1.3 Customer Persona

**Primary Customer: "Confident Carla"**
- Age: 28-45
- Size: US 14-28 (UK 18-32)
- Income: $45,000-$120,000/year
- Lifestyle: Professional, body-positive, active on social media
- Pain Points:
  - Limited stylish options in her size
  - Poor fit from mainstream brands
  - Tired of "basic" plus-size offerings
  - Wants sexy AND comfortable
- Shopping Behavior:
  - 70% influenced by social media
  - Researches extensively before purchase
  - Loyal to brands that "get" her
  - Values authenticity and representation

## PART 3: MANUFACTURING & SOURCING

### 3.1 Sourcing Strategy Comparison

| Factor | China | India | Bangladesh |
|--------|-------|-------|------------|
| **Labor Cost/Month** | $370+ | $100-150 | $75-120 |
| **MOQ** | 500-1000 | 1000-2000 | 500-1000 |
| **FOB Price (Basic Bra)** | $3.50-6.00 | $3.00-5.00 | $2.50-4.50 |
| **FOB Price (Premium)** | $8.00-15.00 | $7.00-12.00 | $6.00-10.00 |
| **Quality** | Excellent | Good-Excellent | Good |
| **Lead Time** | 30-45 days | 45-60 days | 45-60 days |
| **Lingerie Expertise** | Excellent | Growing | Good |
| **IP Protection** | Moderate | Better | Limited |
| **US Tariffs** | 15-25% | 0-7.5% | 0-7.5% |

### 3.2 Recommended Strategy: HYBRID APPROACH

**Phase 1 (Launch - 6 months): China**
- Lower MOQs, faster turnaround
- Established lingerie manufacturing ecosystem
- Use Alibaba verified suppliers
- Focus on getting to market quickly

**Recommended Chinese Suppliers (via Alibaba/Jingsourcing):**
- Shantou, Guangdong (lingerie hub)
- Dongguan (premium manufacturing)
- MOQ: 500 pieces per style/color

**Phase 2 (6-18 months): India Partnership**
- Set up production in Mumbai/Tirupur
- Lower landed costs
- Family business advantage
- "Made in India" appeal for certain markets

**Indian Lingerie Hubs:**
- **Mumbai** - Your home base, good for oversight
- **Tirupur, Tamil Nadu** - India's knitwear capital
- **Surat, Gujarat** - Textile hub
- **Noida/NCR** - Growing lingerie cluster

**Phase 3 (18+ months): Multi-Source**
- China for fashion/trend pieces
- India for core basics
- Bangladesh for high-volume basics (if scale justifies)

### 3.3 Quality Control Process

1. **Pre-Production:**
   - Fit sample approval (3 rounds)
   - Fabric testing (stretch recovery, wash durability)
   - Size grading verification

2. **Production:**
   - AQL 2.5 inspection standard
   - In-line inspection at 30%, 60%, 90%
   - Color matching (Pantone verification)

3. **Pre-Shipment:**
   - Final random inspection
   - Measurement checks (all sizes)
   - Metal detection (bra wires)
   - Packaging verification

## PART 5: SALES CHANNELS

### 5.1 Channel Strategy

**Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)**
- 60% Amazon FBA
- 40% Shopify Website

**Phase 2: Growth (Months 6-12)**
- 50% Amazon
- 40% Website
- 10% Wholesale inquiries

**Phase 3: Scale (Year 2+)**
- 40% Amazon
- 45% Website
- 15% Wholesale/B2B

### 5.2 Amazon FBA Strategy

**Category Selection:**
- Primary: Clothing, Shoes & Jewelry > Women > Lingerie, Sleep & Lounge > Lingerie
- Secondary: Plus-Size specific categories

**Listing Requirements:**
- Professional product photography (white background)
- Lifestyle images (model shots - Amazon compliant)
- Detailed size charts
- A+ Content (Brand Registry required)

**Amazon Restrictions for Intimate Apparel:**
- No cropped images focusing on intimate areas
- No transparent garments showing body parts
- No models in unnatural settings
- Opaque poly bags required for FBA
- Adult products need special approval

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Price 5-10% below Savage X Fenty
- Premium to Amazon basics
- Use coupons for launch velocity

**Launch Tactics:**
- Amazon Vine program for initial reviews
- PPC campaigns (Sponsored Products)
- External traffic from social media
- Lightning deals for visibility

## PART 7: OPERATIONS

### 7.1 Fulfillment Strategy

**Amazon Orders:** FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon)
- Ship inventory to Amazon warehouses
- They handle pick, pack, ship
- Prime badge for customers
- Higher fees, lower headache

**Website Orders:** 3PL Partnership
- Recommended: ShipBob, Deliverr, or local 3PL
- Per-order fees: $3-5 + shipping
- Start with self-fulfillment if <50 orders/day

### 7.2 Customer Service

**Channels:**
- Email (primary)
- Instagram DMs
- Chat widget on website

**Response Time Goals:**
- Email: < 24 hours
- Social: < 4 hours
- Chat: < 2 minutes

**Common Issues (Pre-plan Responses):**
- Size exchanges (free first exchange)
- Returns (30-day policy)
- Fit questions (size guide)
- Shipping inquiries (tracking)

### 7.3 Returns Policy

- 30-day return window
- Unworn, tags attached
- Free return shipping (US)
- Exchange or refund
- Expected return rate: 15-25% (lingerie industry standard)
