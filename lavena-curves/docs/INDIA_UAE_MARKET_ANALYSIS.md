### 3.1 INDIA - Online Marketplaces

| **Amazon India** | 17-20% | Largest reach, FBA available | High competition, strict policies | **#1** |

| **Nykaa Fashion** | 18-25% | Growing fast, beauty + fashion | Smaller but targeted | Consider |

#### Amazon India Strategy

**Category:** Clothing & Accessories > Women > Lingerie > Bras

**Fees Breakdown:**
| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Referral Fee | 17% |
| Closing Fee | ₹10-30 per item |
| FBA Fee | ₹50-80 per item |
| **Total** | ~22-25% of selling price |

**Example Unit Economics (₹1,200 bra):**
| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Selling Price | ₹1,200 |
| Amazon Fees (22%) | ₹264 |
| Landed Cost | ₹400 |
| Packaging | ₹30 |
| **Gross Profit** | **₹506 (42%)** |

**Myntra Strategy**

**Best for:** Premium positioning, fashion-conscious customers
**Commission:** 4-15% (lower than Amazon!)
**Requirements:**
- GST Registration
- Brand Trademark or Authorization
- VAT Registration
- Quality certification

**Recommendation:** Start Amazon → Add Myntra after 6 months with proven sales

## PART 7: PROFIT MAXIMIZATION STRATEGIES

### 7.1 Working Capital Optimization

| Strategy | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| **Start with bestseller sizes only** | 80/20 rule - 80% of sales from 20% of sizes |
| **Pre-order model for website** | Cash before inventory |
| **Fast restocking of winners** | Keep buffer for hot SKUs |
| **Drop slow movers quickly** | Offer at cost, don't hold |
| **Bundle deals** | Move slow SKUs with fast ones |

### 7.2 Margin Protection

| Tactic | Implementation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Anchor pricing** | Show MRP ₹1,999, sell at ₹1,499 |
| **Bundle for AOV** | Bra + Panty combo at 10% discount |
| **Limit discounts** | Max 20% off, even during sales |
| **Premium packaging** | Justifies premium pricing |
